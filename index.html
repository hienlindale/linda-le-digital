My approach

Content that drives bookings

Great content isn't just pretty — it's strategic. Here's how I approach every salon.

01
Platform-first shooting
I shoot with Instagram and TikTok in mind — vertical framing, close-ups, and motion-ready clips built to perform in the algorithm.
02
Technique & process content
Finished sets attract saves. Process videos attract followers. I capture both — 15–40 second clips that show the skill behind every service.
03
Before & after storytelling
Transformation content is one of the highest-performing formats for salons. Same angle, same framing — so results speak for themselves.
04
Consistent posting rhythm
An active feed signals a trustworthy salon. I build a weekly content calendar so there are never gaps that make your account look inactive.
05
Educational captions
Captions that explain the service position your salon as the local expert. I write copy that educates and sells — without feeling pushy.
06
Analytics & optimisation
Every month I review reach, saves, and profile visits — then refine what we shoot next so your content strategy keeps improving.
